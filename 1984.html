1984

by George Orwell

Genre: Dystopian, Science Fiction, Political Fiction

Rating: ★★★★★

Price: GHS 150 ~ 180

Description: 1984 is a dystopian novel that presents a chilling depiction of a totalitarian regime where truth and freedom are suppressed. It explores the themes of surveillance, propaganda, and the consequences of totalitarianism.

Synopsis: Set in a dystopian future, the story follows Winston Smith, a low-ranking member of the Party in the totalitarian state of Oceania. The Party, led by the omnipresent Big Brother, exercises absolute control over every aspect of life, manipulating truth and history. As Winston becomes increasingly disillusioned with the Party's oppressive rule, he begins a forbidden love affair and starts to question the truth he has been taught. The novel delves into themes of identity, control, and resistance, highlighting the dangers of a society that sacrifices freedom for security.
