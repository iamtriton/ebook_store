The Vanishing Half

by Brit Bennett

Genre: Literary Fiction, Historical Fiction, Family Saga

Rating: ★★★★★

Price: GHS 150 ~ 180

Description: The Vanishing Half tells the story of twin sisters, Desiree and Stella Vignes, who grow up in a racially segregated Southern town. As adults, Desiree returns home, embracing her identity, while Stella passes as white and lives a secret life. The novel explores themes of race, identity, and the far-reaching impact of their choices on their family and future generations.

Synopsis: The Vanishing Half follows the lives of twin sisters Desiree and Stella Vignes, who make drastically different life choices after leaving their racially divided hometown. Desiree embraces her heritage, returning with her daughter, while Stella chooses to pass as white, leading a life of secrecy. The novel delves into the complexities of identity and the impact of the sisters' choices on themselves and their descendants, highlighting the enduring power of family bonds and the quest for personal identity.
