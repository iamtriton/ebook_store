The Alchemist

by Paulo Coelho

Genre: Fiction, Adventure, Philosophy

Rating: ★★★★☆

Price: GHS 150 ~ 180

Description: The Alchemist is a philosophical tale about a young Andalusian shepherd named Santiago who embarks on a journey to find a worldly treasure, only to discover a profound spiritual understanding of life and the importance of following one's dreams.

Synopsis: Santiago's journey begins with a recurring dream about finding treasure in the Egyptian pyramids. Along the way, he encounters a series of mentors and challenges that teach him important life lessons. The story is a metaphorical exploration of personal growth and the pursuit of one's destiny, encouraging readers to listen to their hearts and follow their dreams.
